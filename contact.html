FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a project quote?

For standard service requests, we typically provide a detailed quotation within 3–5 working days of receiving your enquiry. For complex multi-disciplinary projects that require site visits and detailed estimation, this may take up to 10 working days. We always aim to be as prompt as possible.
Do you take up projects outside Pune?

Yes. We operate across Maharashtra, with active projects in Pune, Mumbai, Nashik, Kolhapur, Aurangabad, and Satara. For large-scale or long-duration projects, we also consider opportunities in neighbouring states such as Goa, Karnataka, and Gujarat.
What is included in your Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

Our AMC packages are customised to each client's asset base and criticality. A standard AMC includes scheduled preventive maintenance visits (monthly or quarterly), consumable replacement, condition monitoring reports, priority response for breakdowns, and a dedicated service engineer contact. We also offer comprehensive AMCs that include parts and labour for agreed equipment.
Are you registered and compliant with statutory requirements?

Yes. Hari Krupa Engineering holds ISO 9001:2015 certification, is registered as a Licensed Electrical Contractor with the Maharashtra Electrical Licensing Board, is a registered vendor with MIDC, and holds all applicable factory, labour, and contractor registrations required under Maharashtra and Central government regulations.
