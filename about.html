Our Company

Built on a Foundation of Trust & Precision

Hari Krupa Engineering was founded in 2009 by Mr. Harish Patel — a mechanical engineer with a decade of industry experience and a clear vision: to build an engineering company that would set new standards for quality, reliability, and innovation in the Indian industrial sector.

What began as a three-person team operating out of a 400 sq.ft. workshop in MIDC Pune has grown into a full-spectrum engineering firm employing over 80 qualified engineers and technicians. Today, we operate across Maharashtra with project sites spanning Pune, Mumbai, Nashik, Kolhapur, and Aurangabad.

Our growth has been driven not by aggressive sales, but by the trust of repeat clients. Over 70% of our annual project volume comes from clients who have worked with us before — a statistic we are deeply proud of and committed to maintaining.

We hold ISO 9001:2015 certification for our quality management systems and are registered with the Maharashtra Electrical Licensing Board and various other statutory bodies, ensuring full regulatory compliance across all our projects.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Who We Are

2009
Company Founded
Hari Krupa Engineering established in MIDC Pune by Harish Patel with a team of 3 engineers and a focus on mechanical maintenance services.
2012
Electrical Division Launched
Expanded into electrical engineering services. Secured first major contract — complete electrical installation of a 50,000 sq.ft. automotive component plant in Chakan.
2015
ISO 9001 Certification
Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification — a major milestone affirming our quality management systems. Grew team to 30+ engineers and crossed ₹5 Crore annual turnover.
2018
Automation Division Established
Launched a dedicated Automation & Controls division, investing in PLC/SCADA capabilities. Completed first major Industry 4.0 project for a Pune-based pharmaceutical manufacturer.
2021
Expansion Across Maharashtra
Opened satellite offices in Mumbai and Nashik. Completed 300th project milestone. Upgraded to ISO 9001:2015. Launched AMC division for preventive maintenance services.
2024
500+ Projects & Growing
Surpassed 500 completed projects with 200+ active clients. 80+ team members. Recognised as a Top Engineering Contractor by the Mahratta Chamber of Commerce. Future plans include expansion into Goa and Karnataka.
